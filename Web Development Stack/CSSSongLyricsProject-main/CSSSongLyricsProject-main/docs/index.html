About the Song

Jvke's 'Golden Hour,' released in July 2022, gained TikTok fame and international success. With semi-rapped verses, modern music references, and a compelling chorus, the song reached top charts worldwide. Billboard praised its winning formula, featuring a racing piano line and Jvke's emotive vocals. Described as a glittery ballad and a slow-burn love song by NME, the Official Charts Company highlighted its tender piano ballad essence with a hip-hop influence.

About the Artist

Jvke, born Jacob Dodge Lawson in 2001, is an American singer-songwriter, producer, and social media personality. He gained prominence on TikTok during the COVID-19 lockdowns, with his song "Upside Down" going viral in 2021. His debut album, "This Is What ____ Feels Like" (2022), peaked at #40 on the Billboard 200, and the single "Golden Hour" reached #10 on the Billboard Hot 100. Jvke was named MTV Push Artist for October 2022, and he performed "Golden Hour" on the Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon. With a musical upbringing in the church, Jvke's journey includes collaborations, chart-topping hits, and a rapidly growing fan base.
